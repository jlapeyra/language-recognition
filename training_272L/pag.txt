Si Michelangelo di Lodovico Buonarroti (6 Marso 1475 – 18 Pebrero 1564) et sakey ya Italian Renaissance sculptor, painter, architect san poet, ya mas kabat ya Michelangelo. Lapur ed artistic versality to, dakel so mannununot ya sikato so perpekton ehemplo na Renaissance man.
Si Michelangelo et tampol a nagmaliw a bantog; duara ed saray kabkabat ton kimey, say Pietà tan David, et ginawa antis ya mantaon na talomplo. Anggaman agto impasen so inkasikato a sakey a pintor, si Michelangelo so nanggawa na duaran sankamaimpluensyan fresco ed awaran na Bokig ya art: saray eksena manlapud Genesis diad atep na Sistine Chapel ed Roma, tan Say Unor a Panangukom diad dingding na altar na satan. Say desinyo to ed Laurentian Library so nan-pioneer ed Mannerist architecture.[1] Diad edad ton 71, sikatoy sinmalat ed si Antonio da Sangallo ya Mas ugaw bilang arkitekto na San Basilica nen Pedro. Impasimbalo nen Michelangelo so plano ta pian nasumpal so sagur a sampot unong ed desinyo to, a singa met ed dome, a wala so pigaran pananguman, kayari impatey to.
Sakey ya Kastilan nobelista, poet san playwright si Miguel de Cervantes Saavedra (Septyembre 29, 1547 – Abril 23, 1616). Sankakabatan si Cervantes ed nobela ton Don Quixote de la Mancha, ya ibabaga dan sakey ya sankanayarian ya writing ed Western literature, san sankanayarian ed Salitan Kastila.
Sakey ya Chinese thinker, social philosopher san educator si K'ung-Tzu (孔子, Enianak nen 551 BK san enatey nen 479 BK). Sakey met na editer na Chunqiu. Say trabaho to so foundation ya Confucianism (儒家) na Dinastiyang Zhou.
Sakey ya Italyano san Romano Katoliko ya philosopher si Saint Thomas Aquinas [Thomas of Aquin, Aquino, odino Santo Tomas Aquino] (enianak nen 1225 san enatey nen March 7, 1274). Lapor ed saray ensulat to, akabat ya Doctor Angelicus, Doctor Universalis.

Say Dagat Pacifico (e.g. Mare Pacificum ed salitan Latino) sakey pina-dakkel ya dayat so dalin. Kabiangan itan na kakatlo na interon kabaleg na Dalin tan sukat na 179.7 milyon km2 (69.4 milyon a kuwadrado a milya). Manga 15,500 km (9,600 mi) so karukey to manlapud Bering Sea diad Dayat Arktiko ya anggad balbaleg ya Ross Sea ed Antarktika diad abalaten. Walaay bandad bukig-abagatan a sukat na manga 5 grado H latitude, a manga 19,800 km (12,300 mi) so kaarawi to manlapud Indonesia anggad baybay na Colombia. Say western a ketegan na dayat et kaslakan a say Kipot na Malacca. Say sankamelagan a pasen ed mundo et walad saray Marianas Islands diad leksab na Pasipiko. Say Marianas Tren et 10,911 metro (35,797 ft) ed leksab na dayat.
